Ghormesabzi

Description

Ghorme Sabzi is one of the most delicious Iranian khoreshes, stews. This khoresh can be traced back to 1500 BC and it can be found all over the country with small variations, it is usually the first suggestion if you try an Iranian food.

Ingredients

*This amount of ingredients is enough for four to five people

  • lamb 400 gr
  • finely chopped vegetable leaves including cilantro, chives, parsley, fenugreek and spinach 1 kg
  • kidney bean 200 gr
  • dried lime 4
  • onion 1
  • salt and black paper to taste
  • frying oil 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Put the beans in a bowl of water and let them rest for six hours.
  2. Fry the chopped vegetable with one tbsp oil.
  3. Chop finley the onion and fry it with another tbsp oil until becomes golden brown.
  4. Chop lamp in fairly large pieces and add it to the fried onion let it fry until its color changes.
  5. Drain the beans add them to the pan of onion and lamb after one minute add four to six cup of water to them.
  6. Let the combination boil slowly up until the beans and meat are almost cooked.
  7. Add the fried vegetable and dried limes and let it cook completely.
